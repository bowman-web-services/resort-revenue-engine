## Vacation Ownership, Reimagined

The Cove Club is a prepaid-nights membership program. You buy a block of future nights at today's rates. When you're ready to travel, you book online or call our AI concierge, pay a per-person all-inclusive fee, and enjoy VIP treatment.

**No timeshare. No 90-minute presentation. No hidden fees. No 30-year contract.**
