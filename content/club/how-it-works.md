## Step 1: Choose Your Tier

Pick the membership level that matches how often you travel. Explorer (20 nights) for occasional getaways, Voyager (40 nights) for regular vacations, or Connoisseur (70 nights) for the ultimate flexibility.

## Step 2: Lock In Your Rates

Pay your initiation fee upfront or spread it over monthly payments. Your nightly rate is locked for the life of your membership — no surprise price increases on the nights you've already purchased.

## Step 3: Book When You're Ready

Log into the member portal or call Coco, our AI concierge. See real-time availability, pick your dates, and get confirmed in 60 seconds. No calling a reservation line, no waiting days for confirmation, no staff saying "that week isn't available."

## Step 4: Pay Your All-Inclusive Fee

When you travel, you pay a per-person all-inclusive fee that covers meals, drinks, activities, and entertainment. The fee varies by tier — Connoisseur members pay the lowest rates. Kids under 4 are always free.

## Step 5: Enjoy VIP Treatment

Members are recognized from the moment they arrive. Depending on your tier, you'll enjoy priority check-in, à la carte dining, VIP pool and beach sections, premium drinks, spa discounts, and more.
