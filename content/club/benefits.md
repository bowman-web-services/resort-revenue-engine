## The VIP Experience

From the moment you check in, you're recognized as a Cove Club member. Your black bracelet signals to every staff member that you're VIP — and the perks are real.

### Dining

- **Explorer:** International buffet + 1 à la carte dinner per week
- **Voyager:** Unlimited à la carte dining at all restaurants + priority reservations
- **Connoisseur:** All Voyager benefits + private dining experiences + chef's table access

### Drinks

- **Explorer:** Domestic beer, house wine, well cocktails
- **Voyager:** Premium spirits, imported beer, full cocktail menu
- **Connoisseur:** Top-shelf everything — including reserve wines and premium tequilas

### Beach & Pool

- **Explorer:** Priority pool chair reservations
- **Voyager:** VIP pool section + VIP beach area with dedicated service
- **Connoisseur:** All VIP areas + complimentary cabana (subject to availability)

### Spa & Wellness

- **Explorer:** 15% off all spa treatments
- **Voyager:** 15% off + 1 complimentary 30-min treatment per stay
- **Connoisseur:** 25% off + 1 complimentary 60-min treatment per stay

### Golf

- **Explorer & Voyager:** 20% off green fees
- **Connoisseur:** Unlimited golf included

### Transportation

- **Explorer:** Discounted airport transfer ($59/couple vs. $89 retail)
- **Voyager & Connoisseur:** Complimentary round-trip airport transfer

### Booking & Service

- **All tiers:** Real-time online booking + AI concierge (EN/ES)
- **Voyager:** Priority customer service queue
- **Connoisseur:** Dedicated concierge + butler for suites
- **Ambassador:** Personal travel planner + guaranteed upgrades
