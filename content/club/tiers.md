## 🏆 Ambassador Tier

**Invitation only.** After 3+ years as a Connoisseur with 80%+ utilization, you may be invited to Ambassador status. Benefits include waived maintenance fees, $55/adult AI fee, guaranteed room upgrades, annual complimentary week, personal travel planner, and exclusive Ambassador events. This tier is earned — not purchased.

Beyond Mexico's mandatory 5-day cooling-off period, we offer a **30-day satisfaction guarantee**. If you join The Cove Club and aren't satisfied after your first stay, cancel within 30 days for a full refund minus any all-inclusive fees consumed. No other program offers this.
