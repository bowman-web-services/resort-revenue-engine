## Turn Unused Nights Into Value

*In a real implementation, this page would be behind a member login and feature a calendar interface for selecting available dates, setting prices, and managing listings.*

### Listing Requirements

- Must be a Voyager or Connoisseur member (Explorer nights are non-transferable)
- Minimum 3-night listing
- Price must be between AI fee cost (floor) and 80% of retail OTA rate (ceiling)
- Resort approval required (24-hour turnaround)
- Maximum 4 weeks listed per year (Connoisseur) or 2 weeks (Voyager)

### Commission Structure

The resort takes a 15% commission on completed rentals. This covers:
- Payment processing and guest billing
- Guest vetting and communication
- Check-in coordination and bracelet issuance
- Quality assurance and dispute resolution

### Listing Tips

- List at least 60 days before the dates for best visibility
- Price competitively — guests compare against OTA rates
- Holiday weeks and high season command premium pricing
- Include a personal note about why you love these dates
