## For Members: Listing Your Weeks

1. **Log in** to the member portal
2. **Select dates** you want to make available
3. **Set your price** (minimum: AI fee cost; maximum: 80% of retail OTA rate)
4. **Submit for approval** — resort reviews within 24 hours
5. **Once approved** — listing goes live on the portal
6. **When booked** — you receive your rental payment minus 15% resort commission

## For Guests: Booking Member Rates

1. **Browse** available member weeks on the portal
2. **Select dates** and room type
3. **Book and pay** through the official portal
4. **Receive confirmation** — same-day, all details included
5. **Check in** with your "Member Guest" bracelet

## Member Guest Benefits

Guests who book through the rental portal receive:
- All-inclusive access (same as members)
- "Member Guest" bracelet with defined perks
- Access to most member amenities (not VIP areas)
- Booking confirmation and support from the resort
- Quality-guaranteed experience

## Revenue Split

| Party | Gets |
|-------|------|
| Member | Rental price minus 15% commission |
| Resort | 15% commission + all-inclusive fees from guest |
| Guest | Member-quality experience at below-retail rates |
