## Don't Let Your Weeks Go to Waste

<!-- IMAGE: member-rentals-portal.jpg -->

Life happens. Sometimes you can't use all your Cove Club nights. Instead of letting them expire, share them through our **official Member Rental Portal** — the resort-sanctioned way to transfer or rent your weeks.

### Why Use the Official Portal?

| | Rogue Rental Sites | Official Portal |
|---|---|---|
| Resort quality control | ❌ None | ✅ Resort-approved |
| Guest vetting | ❌ None | ✅ Verified guests |
| Billing & disputes | ❌ Your problem | ✅ Resort-managed |
| Member perks for guests | ❌ Not honored | ✅ "Member Guest" bracelet |
| Resort commission | ❌ $0 to resort | ✅ 15% (everyone wins) |
| Brand protection | ❌ Damages brand | ✅ Enhances brand |

<div class="coco-says">

We built this portal because members at other resorts were creating unauthorized rental websites — damaging the resort brand and creating quality control nightmares. This keeps everyone happy: members earn credit, guests get a great experience, and the resort stays in control.
