## Why Book Through the Official Portal

Cove Club members sometimes have weeks they can't use. Instead of those weeks going to waste — or being sold through unauthorized third-party sites — they list them here on the official resort portal.

**What that means for you:**
- **Below-retail pricing** — Member rates are significantly less than OTA rates
- **Resort-backed guarantee** — Your booking is managed by the resort, not a stranger on the internet
- **Member Guest perks** — You get a "Member Guest" bracelet with all-inclusive benefits
- **Quality assurance** — Same room quality, same service, same experience
