### 🌅 Beach Gazebo
Toes-in-the-sand ceremony with a white-draped gazebo and Caribbean sunset backdrop. Capacity: 20-80 guests. Best for: Intimate ceremonies and sunset weddings.

### 🌿 Garden Terrace
Lush tropical garden with a stone pathway, flowering archway, and fountain. Capacity: 30-100 guests. Best for: Romantic garden weddings and evening receptions.

### 🌊 Overwater Deck
Elevated wooden platform extending over the turquoise water. Capacity: 20-60 guests. Best for: Intimate luxury ceremonies with ocean views.

### 🎩 Grand Ballroom
Air-conditioned, elegantly appointed with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the ocean. Capacity: 50-150 guests. Best for: Grand celebrations and formal receptions.

### 🌴 Private Island Dinner
Exclusive use of our offshore sandbar — accessible by boat. Capacity: 2-20 guests. Best for: Ultra-intimate elopements and rehearsal dinners.
