## Our Commitment

Coconut Cove is committed to providing an exceptional vacation experience for every guest, regardless of ability. We comply with applicable accessibility standards and continually improve our facilities.

### Accessible Accommodations
- 18 fully accessible rooms across all categories
- Roll-in showers with grab bars and shower seats
- Lowered countertops, closet rods, and light switches
- Visual fire alarms and door knock alerts
- Accessible balconies with wider doorways

### Property Accessibility
- Wheelchair-accessible pathways throughout the resort
- Elevator access to all floors in all buildings
- Accessible pool entry (pool lifts at all 3 pools)
- Beach wheelchairs available (complimentary, first-come)
- Accessible restrooms in all public areas

### Dining
- Wheelchair-accessible seating at all restaurants
- Braille menus available upon request
- Dietary accommodations for medical needs
- Staff trained in allergen awareness

### Services
- Service animals welcome in all areas
- Assistive listening devices available for shows
- Large-print materials available
- TTY/TDD available at front desk
- Sign language interpretation (72-hour advance notice)

### Transportation
- Wheelchair-accessible airport transfer vehicles
- Accessible resort shuttle

### Request Accommodations

Contact us at least 72 hours before arrival to arrange specific accommodations. Our accessibility coordinator will work with you to ensure a comfortable stay.
