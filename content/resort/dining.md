## Your Table Is Ready

<!-- IMAGE: dining-overview-collage.jpg -->

### Restaurants

🍳 **The Coconut Kitchen** — International Buffet
Breakfast, lunch, and dinner with live cooking stations. Asian, Mexican, Italian, Caribbean, and American selections daily. Open seating, family-friendly.

🐟 **Coral Reef** — Seafood · À la carte
Fresh-caught fish, ceviche, grilled lobster, and ocean-to-table specialties. Oceanfront terrace dining. *Voyager+ members: unlimited access. Explorer: 1 visit/week.*

🌮 **Cocina Maya** — Mexican & Yucatecan · À la carte
Authentic regional cuisine — mole, cochinita pibil, handmade tortillas, premium tequila and mezcal selection. Indoor and garden patio seating.

🥩 **The Cove Club Steakhouse** — Premium Steaks · À la carte
Dry-aged cuts, craft cocktails, sommelier wine pairings. The most exclusive dining experience at the resort. *Connoisseur+ members: priority reservations.*

### Bars & Casual

🍹 **Coco's Beach Bar** — Beachfront
Signature coconut cocktails, cold beer, fresh juices. Toes in the sand. Open 10am–sunset.

🎵 **The Lobby Lounge** — Premium Bar
Live music Thursday–Sunday. Top-shelf spirits, classic cocktails, fine wine. Members enjoy premium pour at every bar.

🏊 **Swim-Up Bar** — Pool Bar
Never leave the pool. Frozen drinks, light bites, tropical beats.

🥤 **Juice Shack** — Health Bar
Fresh smoothies, acai bowls, cold-pressed juices, energy bites. Open 7am–6pm.

### Dietary Accommodations

We take dietary needs seriously. Vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, kosher-style, halal, and allergen-aware options are available at every restaurant. Notify us during booking and we'll prepare for your arrival.
