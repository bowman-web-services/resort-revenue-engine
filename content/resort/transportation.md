## Airport Transfers

**Nearest Airport:** Cancun International (CUN) — approximately 45 minutes

| Service | Price | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Resort shuttle (shared) | $49/person | Scheduled departures every 2 hours |
| Private transfer | $89/couple | Direct, no stops |
| VIP transfer (Suburban) | $140/couple | Drinks, snacks, dedicated host |
| Cove Club Voyager+ | **Complimentary** | Private transfer included |

### Shuttle Service

Complimentary resort shuttle runs to Playa del Carmen (15 min) and Cancun Hotel Zone (40 min) daily. Check the activity desk for schedules.

### Rental Cars

Hertz and Europcar desks in the lobby. Members receive 15% off rental rates.

### Taxis & Ride-share

Official taxi stand at the resort entrance. Uber and DiDi available in the region. Resort concierge can arrange all ground transportation.
