## Your Sanctuary Awaits

<!-- IMAGE: spa-treatment-room.jpg -->

Spa del Coco is a 15,000 sq ft wellness sanctuary featuring indoor and outdoor treatment rooms, hydrotherapy circuits, a traditional temazcal (Mayan steam lodge), and a tranquil relaxation garden.

### Signature Treatments

💆 **Coconut Bliss Massage** (60/90 min) — $120/$160
Warm coconut oil, hot stones, and deep-tissue techniques.

🌺 **Caribbean Glow Facial** (75 min) — $140
Tropical fruit enzymes, collagen mask, and LED therapy.

🥥 **Coco Body Wrap** (90 min) — $180
Full-body coconut and honey wrap with scalp massage.

👫 **Couples Cove Retreat** (120 min) — $380/couple
Side-by-side massage, facial, champagne, chocolate-dipped fruit.

### Hydrotherapy Circuit

Included for all spa guests: hot tub, cold plunge, steam room, sauna, vitality pool, experience showers, and relaxation lounge with herbal teas.

### Temazcal Ceremony

Traditional Mayan purification ritual led by a certified guide. Available Wednesday and Saturday evenings. Max 12 guests. $75/person.
