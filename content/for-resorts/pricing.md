## Additional Revenue Streams for BWS Partners

Beyond the platform fee, we earn when your resort earns:

- **Member acquisition referral**: Per-member fee for each membership we help close
- **Revenue share**: Percentage of AI fees from members we source
- **SEO/Content retainer**: Ongoing content and optimization
- **AI agent management**: Monthly management of phone/chat agents

This means **our incentives align with yours** — we make more when your resort makes more.
