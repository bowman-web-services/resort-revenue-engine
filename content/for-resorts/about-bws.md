## The Short Version

Bowman Web Services is a one-person digital agency based in Port Orange, Florida, with 28+ years of business experience and a portfolio of 10+ websites across travel, hospitality, and local services.

**Tom Bowman** (that's me) builds, manages, and optimizes digital revenue platforms for businesses that need real results — not agency overhead.

## Why This Works

Traditional agencies charge $10K/month and assign your account to a junior coordinator. I charge a fraction of that and do the work myself — with AI as my force multiplier.

- **Hugo static sites** load faster and cost less than WordPress
- **Telnyx AI agents** answer phones 24/7 for $0.10/minute instead of $20/hour
- **N8N automation** replaces manual workflows that burn staff time
- **Airtable CRM** gives you visibility without Salesforce complexity

The result: **agency-quality output at freelancer pricing**, backed by decades of business experience.
