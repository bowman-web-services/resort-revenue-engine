## What Powers the Resort Revenue Engine

Every component of our platform is chosen for speed, reliability, and cost-effectiveness. No bloated enterprise software. No six-figure licensing fees. Just modern tools that work.

### Websites — Hugo + Netlify

**Static sites that load in under 1 second.** Hugo generates lightning-fast HTML from templates — no WordPress databases to hack, no PHP servers to crash. Hosted on Netlify's global CDN with automatic SSL, deployment from GitHub, and 99.99% uptime.

- Page load time: < 1 second (vs. 3-5 seconds for typical resort sites)
- SEO-optimized architecture from the ground up
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Multi-language support (EN/ES ready)
- Zero server maintenance

### AI Agents — Telnyx

**Phone and chat agents that speak your guests' language.** Powered by Telnyx's voice AI platform, our agents handle reservations, membership inquiries, wedding coordination, and guest services — 24/7, in English and Spanish.

- Bilingual (EN/ES) phone and chat agents
- 24/7 availability — no missed calls, no hold times
- Cost: $0.05-$0.15 per minute (vs. $3-$5 for human agents)
- Call transcripts logged to CRM automatically
- Human escalation when needed

### Automation — N8N

**Workflows that run while you sleep.** N8N connects every system — website forms, CRM, email, phone agents, payment processing — into automated workflows that handle lead routing, follow-ups, confirmations, and reporting.

- Lead form → CRM record → assignment → follow-up email (under 5 minutes)
- Booking confirmation sequences
- Wedding inquiry → packet delivery → coordinator assignment
- Membership application processing
- Day pass booking + upsell triggers

### CRM — Airtable

**Simple, visual lead management.** No enterprise CRM complexity. Airtable gives your team a clear view of every lead, member, wedding inquiry, and promo prospect — with automated status updates from N8N workflows.

- Lead pipelines by division (booking, wedding, membership, promo, day pass)
- Member management (tier, usage, payments, renewal dates)
- Reporting dashboards
- Mobile access for resort staff

### Hosting & Deployment

- **Netlify** — Global CDN, auto-deploy from GitHub, form handling
- **GitHub** — Version control, collaboration, deployment pipeline
- **Fastmail** — Custom email per property (reservations@, weddings@, club@)
- **Hostinger VPS** — N8N automation server
