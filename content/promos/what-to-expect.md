## No Surprises. Here's Exactly What Happens.

### Your Stay

You'll enjoy a full all-inclusive vacation. Same rooms, same restaurants, same pools, same beach, same entertainment as every other guest. The only difference? You got here at a fraction of the price.

### The Club Overview

During your stay, we'll invite you to a **60-minute overview of The Cove Club** — our membership program. Here's what that looks like:

- **When:** Usually day 2 or 3 of your stay, at a time you choose
- **Duration:** 60 minutes. Not 90. Not 120. Not "until you say yes." Sixty minutes.
- **Where:** The Cove Club lounge — comfortable seating, refreshments, ocean view
- **What:** A membership advisor will explain the program, the tiers, the pricing, and the benefits
- **After:** You'll be invited to join. If you want to, great. If not, **you shake hands and go back to the pool.**

### What We Won't Do

- No high-pressure tactics
- No "let me get my manager" escalation loops
- No guilt trips
- No bait-and-switch on pricing
- No penalty for saying no
- No follow-up harassment after your trip

### Why This Works Better

We pre-qualify guests before they arrive. By the time you sit down for the overview, you already know what The Cove Club is. That means the conversation is informed, relaxed, and productive — for both of us.

<div class="coco-says">

We believe that if the program is genuinely good, we don't need to pressure anyone into buying it. The value speaks for itself. And if it doesn't — that's our problem to fix, not yours.
