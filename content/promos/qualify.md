## Qualification Concept

*In a real implementation, this page would feature an interactive quiz that pre-qualifies prospects before offering discounted rates. Here's what the process would look like:*

### The 10-Question Quiz

1. How many vacations do you take per year?
2. What's your preferred destination type? (Beach, Mountain, City, Adventure)
3. Have you visited an all-inclusive resort before?
4. How many people typically travel with you?
5. What age range are the travelers? (Adults, kids, teens)
6. What's your typical vacation budget per trip?
7. How far in advance do you usually plan vacations?
8. Have you ever been a member of a vacation club? (Yes/No)
9. What matters most to you? (Price, Quality, Convenience, Variety)
10. Are you willing to attend a 60-minute resort overview during your stay?

### Why We Pre-Qualify

Traditional promo/minivac programs bring anyone with a pulse to the resort. Result: low close rates, frustrated guests, and wasted resort resources.

Our approach filters out freeloader traffic by requiring genuine engagement. Prospects who qualify have:
- Demonstrated travel interest and budget
- Consumed educational content about the resort
- Understood that a club overview is part of the experience
- Self-selected based on lifestyle fit

**Result:** Higher close rates, happier guests, lower cost-per-tour, better ROI for the resort.
