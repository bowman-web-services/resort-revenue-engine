## Your Best Port Day Ever

While 3,000+ passengers fight for beach chairs at the public beach, you'll be sipping cocktails at our swim-up bar with a reserved spot in paradise.

### How It Works

1. **Book before you dock** — Reserve online at least 24 hours before your port day
2. **Shuttle picks you up** — Our branded shuttle meets you at the cruise terminal
3. **Wristband on, shoes off** — Check in at our Day Pass welcome desk
4. **Enjoy everything** — Pools, beach, restaurants, bars, water park — it's all yours
5. **Shuttle back** — We'll get you back to the ship with time to spare

### Pricing

Same as standard day pass rates. Shuttle from cruise terminal: **$15/person round-trip** (free for VIP Day pass holders).

### Important for Cruise Guests

- Book early — we cap day pass guests at 50/day to protect the experience
- Last shuttle back departs 90 minutes before ship departure
- Lockers available for luggage ($10/day)
- Shore excursion credit may apply — check with your cruise line
